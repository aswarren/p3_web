Pan-genome Graphs

Create a pan-genome graph from selected genomes or groups.
WARNING: Be mindful of how many completely unrelated genomes you put in (4 at most).
Think of doing an MSA of unrelated sequences (not very informative).
e.g. 10 unrelated genomes x ~ 4,000 genes = 40,000 seperate nodes (will probably break your browser).
The more closely related the genomes the more you can put in. e.g. 400 well conserved Brucella.
